Agile Talent EQ

Overview

This survey is intended to identify how your organization works with external (agile) talent. If you are an internal organization leader, the results of the survey will help you improve how you manage this talent. If you are an external resource, this survey provides information about how effectively you are engaged while working with a specific client organization. There are no right or wrong answers. The survey results will provide comparative information about how agile talent is aligned and engaged along with the implications of that approach.

Instructions

If you are a business leader, please complete the survey about your organization (“this organization”). If you are a consultant/external resource (agile talent), please complete the survey with one specific client organization (“this organization”) in mind. Please answer each question.
